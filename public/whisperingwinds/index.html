Whispering Winds

An international Rotaract Club Newsletter with the motto, "Thoughts transcending borders", it strives to take the Rotaract objective to promote better relations between all people worldwide through a framework of friendship and service to heart.The newsletter aims to connect the youth of different nations by setting up channels of communication to promote goodwill and understanding. Through articles from members of participating clubs, it seeks to erode one's reluctance to share one's thoughts on a public forum while at the same time, instilling in one a feeling of confidence and togetherness.
